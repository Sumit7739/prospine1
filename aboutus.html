Pro Spine: From Vision to Reality

For years, spinal patients had limited options—either physiotherapy or high-risk spinal surgery. This gap led me, Dr. Pranav Kumar, to seek a better solution. With 21 years of experience and treating 50,000+ patients under Manipal Physiotherapy and Fitness Centre, my passion for spine care deepened. Despite mastering Maitland Mobilization, Cyriax Therapy, Mulligan Concept, and McKenzie Method, I found that conventional physiotherapy was sometimes insufficient for severe spinal conditions.

The Turning Point

Spinal disorders often reached a stage where surgery seemed the only option. Recognizing the need for a non-surgical alternative, I researched global advancements and discovered a revolutionary, non-invasive technology. However, it was too expensive for the Indian market. In 2023, an Indian company made this technology affordable, and by April 2024, I installed India’s 3rd and Bihar’s 1st machine, marking the birth of Pro Spine.

Birth of Pro Spine

Pro Spine is not just about one machine—it is a comprehensive non-surgical spinal care protocol. It integrates Shock Wave Therapy, Laser Therapy, Combination Therapy, and Advanced Manual Therapy Techniques. This holistic approach has successfully treated conditions like disc herniation, degenerative disc disease, facet joint syndrome, spondylolisthesis, and spinal canal stenosis.

Transforming Lives

With 100+ patients regaining spinal health without surgery, Pro Spine is pioneering Revolutionizing Non-Surgical Spinal Care in India. Today, it stands as a trusted name in non-invasive spinal treatment, reshaping the future of spinal healthcare.
