Conditions We Treat

We specialize in treating various spinal conditions using non-surgical methods for effective and lasting relief.

Disc Prolapse

A slipped or bulging disc that causes nerve compression, leading to pain and discomfort.

Herniated Disc

When the inner gel-like material of a spinal disc pushes out, causing nerve irritation and pain.

Degenerative Disc

Age-related wear and tear of spinal discs, leading to reduced flexibility and chronic pain.

Retrolisthesis

A condition where a vertebra slips backward, often causing spinal instability and pain.

Facet Joint Syndrome

Arthritis-like condition affecting the small joints in the spine, causing stiffness and discomfort.

Spinal Canal Stenosis

Narrowing of the spinal canal, putting pressure on nerves and causing pain, numbness, or weakness.

Sciatica

Pain radiating along the sciatic nerve due to nerve compression, commonly from a herniated disc.

Scoliosis

Abnormal sideways curvature of the spine that can cause pain and posture-related issues.

Brachial Neuralgia

Nerve pain in the neck, shoulder, and arm caused by compression or irritation of cervical nerves.

Stiff Neck & Back Pain

Muscle tension, poor posture, or spinal issues causing persistent pain and restricted movement.

Muscle Strain

Overuse or sudden movements causing muscle tears, leading to pain and stiffness.

Radiculopathy

Compression of spinal nerve roots leading to pain, weakness, or numbness in the limbs.

Thecal Compression

Pressure on the thecal sac (spinal cord covering), leading to neurological symptoms and pain.

Whiplash Injury

Neck injury caused by sudden jerking movements, often due to car accidents.

Spondylolisthesis

Forward displacement of a vertebra, causing instability and lower back pain.
